About Truthlense

Truthlense is a free tool designed to help you identify bias in articles and news stories. Our goal is to promote critical thinking and media literacy by providing users with insights into the content they consume.

How It Works

Simply paste the text of an article into our analyzer, and Truthlense will evaluate it for potential biases, including political bias, sensationalism, and more. You'll receive a detailed report highlighting any areas of concern.
